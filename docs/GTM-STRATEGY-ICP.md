## Ideal Customer Profiles

### ICP 1: Marketing-First Merchants (Primary Target)

**Demographics:**
- GMV: $50K-500K/year
- Team size: 1-5 people
- Currently spending $200-500/mo on Shopify marketing apps

**Current Stack:**
- Shopify Basic/Standard
- Klaviyo ($45-150/mo)
- Abandoned cart app
- Reviews app
- SMS marketing app
- Potentially 5-10 apps total

**Pain Points:**
- "I'm paying for 8 apps to do what should be standard"
- App conflicts and compatibility issues
- Multiple dashboards to manage
- Hidden costs adding up

**Buying Triggers:**
- App renewal notices
- Shopify price increase
- App breaking after update
- Hitting app limits

**Message:** "All your marketing tools, built-in. No apps, no extra fees."

**Channels to Reach:**
- SEO: "Shopify app alternatives", "Klaviyo alternatives"
- Reddit: r/shopify, r/ecommerce
- Facebook Groups: Shopify entrepreneurs
- YouTube: Shopify tutorial channels

---

### ICP 2: Multi-Channel Sellers

**Demographics:**
- GMV: $100K-1M/year
- Selling on 2-4 platforms (own store + Amazon + eBay + Instagram)
- Team size: 2-10 people

**Current Stack:**
- Shopify + marketplace connector apps
- Separate Amazon Seller Central
- Separate eBay seller account
- Manual inventory syncing or paid tools

**Pain Points:**
- "Managing 3 platforms is killing my margins"
- Inventory sync errors causing overselling
- Different order management systems
- Feed management complexity

**Buying Triggers:**
- Oversold inventory incident
- Marketplace suspension scare
- Channel expansion plans
- Peak season preparation

**Message:** "One platform, every channel. Sync products to Amazon, eBay, Instagram automatically."

**Channels to Reach:**
- Amazon seller forums
- eBay seller communities
- Multi-channel commerce conferences
- Podcast sponsorships (e-commerce focused)

---

### ICP 3: B2B / Wholesale Merchants

**Demographics:**
- GMV: $200K-2M/year
- Mix of B2B and B2C sales
- Sales team of 2-5 people
- Need custom pricing, quotes, account management

**Current Stack:**
- Shopify + B2B app workarounds
- Separate CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Manual quote processes
- Wholesale-specific apps

**Pain Points:**
- "Shopify is B2C-focused, we need B2B workflows"
- No native deal tracking or pipeline management
- Customer-specific pricing is clunky
- Sales team has no visibility into orders

**Buying Triggers:**
- Losing deals due to slow quote process
- CRM integration breaking
- Expanding wholesale operations
- Need for sales team accountability

**Message:** "E-commerce + CRM built for wholesale and B2B."

**Channels to Reach:**
- B2B e-commerce events
- LinkedIn (wholesale, manufacturing)
- Trade publication advertising
- Industry-specific associations

---

### ICP 4: Tech-Savvy SMBs Who Want Control

**Demographics:**
- GMV: $100K-500K/year
- Technical founder or operator
- Frustrated by platform limitations
- Values customization and ownership

**Current Stack:**
- Shopify with custom theme modifications
- Multiple app integrations
- Possibly considering headless or self-hosted

**Pain Points:**
- "Every custom feature costs me $500+ from a developer"
- Locked into Shopify's ecosystem
- Can't access underlying data easily
- App approval and dependency risks

**Buying Triggers:**
- Large development quote for simple feature
- App removed from Shopify app store
- Need for unique functionality
- Data portability concerns

**Message:** "Build plugins with AI. No code, no devs, no limits."

**Channels to Reach:**
- Hacker News, Indie Hackers
- Technical founder communities
- Developer-focused podcasts
- Product Hunt

**Comparison Content:**

**Tutorial Content:**
- "Build a Custom Plugin in 10 Minutes with AI"
- "Set Up Abandoned Cart Recovery (No App Needed)"
- "Connect Your Store to Instagram Shopping in 5 Minutes"

### 2. Migration & Switching Campaign

**Tools to Build:**
- Shopify cost calculator (shows true app stack cost)
- "Calculate your real Shopify cost" interactive widget
- Migration assistant / one-click import from Shopify
- Side-by-side feature comparison tool

**Incentives:**
- Free migration service
- Extended trial for Shopify migrants
- Match remaining Shopify subscription period
- White-glove onboarding for high-value prospects

### 3. Community & Developer Strategy

**Plugin Ecosystem:**
- Plugin marketplace with revenue share for developers
- "Build once, earn forever" developer program
- Highlight AI plugin builder as key innovation
- Developer documentation and API access

**Community Building:**
- Discord/Slack community for merchants
- User-generated plugin showcase
- Case study program with successful merchants
- Merchant referral program

### 4. Partnerships

**Integration Partners:**
- Shipping providers (ShipStation, Shippo)
- Payment processors
- Accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Fulfillment centers (ShipBob, Deliverr)

**Agency Partners:**
- E-commerce agencies tired of Shopify limitations
- Marketing agencies looking for simpler client setups
- Development agencies wanting to offer custom solutions

## Messaging Framework

### Tagline Options

1. "E-commerce, Complete."
2. "Everything Built-In. Nothing Extra."
3. "The All-In-One Commerce Platform"
4. "Stop Paying for Apps. Start Selling."

### Elevator Pitch

> "Catalyst is the e-commerce platform with everything built-in. While Shopify charges you for the platform, then for apps, then for transactions - Catalyst includes email marketing, automations, multi-channel selling, and even an AI plugin builder. One price, everything included."

### Key Messages by ICP

| ICP | Primary Message | Supporting Points |
|-----|-----------------|-------------------|
| Marketing-First | "All your marketing tools, built-in" | RFM scoring, email automation, segments, abandoned cart |
| Multi-Channel | "One platform, every channel" | Amazon, eBay, Instagram native sync |
| B2B/Wholesale | "E-commerce + CRM for B2B" | Deals, pipelines, custom pricing, sales tracking |
| Tech-Savvy | "Build what you need with AI" | Plugin IDE, no developers needed, full control |

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Shopify has more apps" | "You won't need them. Everything's built-in. Check our feature comparison." |
| "My team knows Shopify" | "Same concepts, cleaner interface. Most teams are productive in days, not weeks." |
| "Shopify is the industry standard" | "So was Magento. Standards change when something better comes along." |
| "What about integrations?" | "We integrate with all major tools, plus you can build custom plugins yourself." |
| "Is it stable/reliable?" | "[Share uptime stats, customer testimonials, case studies]" |

## Open Questions

Before finalizing strategy, clarify:

### Pricing
- [ ] What is the pricing model? (Flat fee / Per transaction / Usage-based / Tiered)
- [ ] Are there transaction fees? If so, what percentage?
- [ ] Is there a free tier or trial period?

### Current Traction
- [ ] How many merchants are currently using Catalyst?
- [ ] What segments are they in? (Which ICP?)
- [ ] What's the average GMV of current customers?
- [ ] What's the primary acquisition channel currently?

### Market
- [ ] Target geography? (US only / Global / Specific regions)
- [ ] Primary language support?
- [ ] Currency support?

### Technical
- [ ] SaaS only or self-hosted option?
- [ ] White-label available for agencies?
- [ ] API access level for developers?

### Competitive
- [ ] Which Shopify alternatives are you seeing most in deals? (BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Wix, etc.)
- [ ] What's the primary reason merchants leave competitors?
