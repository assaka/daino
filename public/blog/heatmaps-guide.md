## ğŸ“š Glossary

**Click Heatmap:** Visual representation of where users click on your page

**Scroll Heatmap:** Shows how far down the page users scroll

**Move Heatmap:** Tracks mouse movements to predict attention

**Rage Click:** Multiple rapid clicks on same spot (indicates frustration)

**Hot Zone:** Areas with high interaction (red/orange on heatmap)

**Cold Zone:** Areas with low/no interaction (blue/white on heatmap)

**Scroll Depth:** Percentage of page height a user scrolled to

**The Fold:** The bottom edge of the visible screen before scrolling

**Above the Fold:** Content visible without scrolling (100% see this)

**Below the Fold:** Content requiring scrolling to see

**Thumb Zone (Mobile):** Area easily reachable with thumb (bottom of screen)

**Session:** One visitor's journey on your site

**Engagement Rate:** Percentage of visitors who interact with elements
